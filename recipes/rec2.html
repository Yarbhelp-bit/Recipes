Molten Spice Cake

Description

An easy flourless cake made as individual servings for each of your guests. A rich chocolate cake with a molten center. An easy Valentine's Day dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs, seperated
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, divided
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 1 teaspon all purpose flour
  • 1 16 ounce choco

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease 6 glass custard cups; place cups on a baking sheet.

  2. Beat egg whites and sugar together in a bowl using an electric mixer until stiff peaks form, 4 to 5 minutes.
